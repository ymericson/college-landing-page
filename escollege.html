Applying as a new student

ES College welcomes applications from students across the United States and around the world. We’re looking for applicants who are committed to their studies and who will thrive in our community—a diverse mix of students with a wide variety of interests, talents and goals.

Our decisions are not based solely on one aspect of your application. Rather, we review your essays, letters of recommendation, extracurricular involvements, and yes, those numbers like GPAs and standardized test scores. Each application is read at least twice, including once by your regional dean. At the end of this process, our goal is to bring together a class of dynamic, diverse and academically engaged students.

This portion of our website is here to provide you with resources, from application checklists to info on financial aid and scholarships. If a question arises that you can’t find the answer to, feel free to contact us anytime.

Don't forget, For Your Application to be Complete You must:

  • Submit Application Essays

    Submit your essays to the campus where you plan to begin studying. Click here to download the essay questions and templates. Please skip this step if yousubmitted your essays electronically when you completed your online application.

  • Request Recommendations

    Download two Recommendation Forms. Complete the upper portion of each form and give it two references. Make sure your references mail the form to the ES College campus where you plan to begin studying. Complete the Signature Page Form and mail it to our campus where you plan to begin studying.

  • Request Official Transcripts

    Send an official transcript from your high school and (if aplicable) college. Contact your counselor or transcript office and tell them to send your transcript(s) to the ES College campus where you plan to begin studying. Click here to download the transcript request form.

  • Pay the Application Fee

    The Application reviewing fee is $50 (payable in U.S. funda only) and is nonrefundable. After completing your online application, you will be prompted for your credit card information.

  • International Students

    International students are a vital part of our culture. Students from around the world come to ES College each year with a shared passion to excel and a common aspiration to become the foremost artists of their generation. We're sensitive to their needs and help them to make the most of their experience. Feel free to check out some International Student FAQs.
